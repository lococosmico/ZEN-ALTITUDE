Nos Formations

Des formations complètes pour maîtriser toutes les techniques d'escalade, de l'assurage aux manipulations multipitch.

Introduction to Climbing

2 days (16 hours)

Discover climbing basics in a safe environment. Learn fundamental techniques, essential equipment usage, and crucial safety rules with our experienced instructors.

Register

Belaying Techniques

1 day (8 hours)

Master various belaying techniques to ensure optimal safety. Intensive training with hands-on practice using different belay devices and safety systems.

Register

Advanced Multipitch

3 days (24 hours)

Advanced training for experienced climbers looking to master multi-pitch techniques. Learn anchor management, rappelling, and handling complex climbing situations.

Register

Sécurité en Milieu Naturel

2 jours (16 heures)

Apprenez à évaluer les risques en milieu naturel et à réagir face aux situations d'urgence. Techniques de secours, évaluation des conditions et prise de décision.

Register
